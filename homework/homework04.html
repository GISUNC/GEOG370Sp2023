Homework 4: Georeference image

Deadline:

hw4

Instructions: Turn in a static map based on an image of UNC that you georeference, an interactive tilemap based on the georeferenced image, and the control points used to georeference the image

  • Watch the tutorial videos on how to georeference an image, make tiles and upload it to GitHub.
  • Use the following data to complete the homework: the High Resolution Orthoimagery (the same as the video example) and a topographical map that has not been georeferenced from 1993 without a reference.
  • Create a map of the georeferenced topographical image, with the high resolution orthoimage in the background. Include the scale, north, title and your name.
  • Convert the georeferenced image to a tile layer, and upload the tiles and HTML to GitHub. Be sure that the map can be seen from any computer connected to the internet, and that the page is not only working locally on your computer.
  • Save the control points (at least 6 points) as a text file and upload them to your GitHub
  • Before the deadline upload to Sakai the link to your tilemap and a link to your control points

What will you turn in before the deadline?

  1. On Sakai, upload a map with the scale, north, title and your name. The map should include the georeferenced topographical map, with the high resolution orthoimage in the background.
